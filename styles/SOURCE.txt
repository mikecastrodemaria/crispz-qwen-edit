Style definitions (sdxl_styles_*.json) are the Fooocus / community SDXL prompt-style
collections (SAI, twri, MRE, diva, marc_k3nt3l, Fooocus), adapted here as Z-Image
prompt templates.

Format per entry: {"name", "prompt" (with a {prompt} placeholder), "negative_prompt"}.
crispz-studio loads every styles/*.json at startup and lets you combine them in the
Styles tab; the selected templates wrap your prompt and their negative_prompts are
merged into the negative prompt.

Credit: Fooocus (https://github.com/lllyasviel/Fooocus) and the original SDXL styles
community collections.
